Connect your Google Sheet so courses and colleges show up for everyone, on every device — not just your browser.

Step 1
Make a copy of the template sheet
Click the link below to open the Google Sheets template. Then go to File → Make a copy to save it to your own Google account.

Open template sheet →
Step 2
Publish your sheet to the web
In your copied sheet, go to File → Share → Publish to web. Select Entire Document and Comma-separated values (.csv). Click Publish. Copy the URL it gives you.
Step 3
Paste your sheet URL here
Paste the CSV publish URL below and click Connect. Your website will start reading from your sheet immediately.
